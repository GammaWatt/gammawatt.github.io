  • Prime numbers must be odd
  • They cannot end in 0, or 5
  • The sum of the digits cannot be equal to a number divisible by 3

The thing about looking for prime numbers is that it's the equivalent of looking for voids, vacancies or holes.

It's one thing to search for something that exists. But searching for prime numbers is the equivalent of trying to have a formula for finding something which doesn't have anything connected to it. You're wanting to find the nodes in a grid that have no edges conencted to them. How do you do this???
